What is Pt?

Pt is a study of Point, Form, and Space. These 3 concepts and their extensions address a common theme: the expressions of a point in a space.

Space defines the context, Point represents a concept, and Form provides an expression of a concept in a context.

Point, Form, and Space here are implemented as base classes in javascript, and are extended to expand its meanings and features. For example, CanvasSpace is a kind of Space that works with html5 canvas, and Line is a Pair of points whose base class is a Point.

Pt is currently in an experimental state. Please send your feedbacks and report issues.
