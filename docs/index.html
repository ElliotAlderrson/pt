What is Pt?

Pt is a study of Point, Form, and Space. These 3 concepts and their extensions address a common theme: the expressions of a point in a space.

Space defines the context, Point represents a concept, and Form provides an expression of a concept in a context. Learn more.

Point, Form, and Space here are implemented as base classes in javascript, and are extended to expand its meanings and features.

Pt is currently in an experimental state. Please send your feedbacks and report issues.
